Sea Salt Vanilla [PackVersion]() for Minecraft [MCVersion]() is a Fabric modpack that improves the player client experience.

## Features

**Mods**

- Performance tuning for smoother frame pacing, lower rendering cost, better memory use, and more responsive client behavior.
- Interface, control, inventory, map, screenshot, accessibility, and multiplayer client improvements for everyday play.
- Animation, camera, item feedback, combat feedback, ambience, audio, particles, and visual polish that make routine actions feel clearer and more complete.

**Resource packs**

- Vanilla-style texture refinements for blocks, items, GUI, HUD elements, and supported client interfaces.
- Entity, player, animation, and localization additions that improve presentation while preserving a familiar Minecraft look.

**Data packs**

- Global world-generation additions for terrain, continents, structures, dungeons, towns, towers, and dimension exploration.
- Pack-level data packs expand exploration without requiring per-world manual setup.

**Shader packs**

- Curated shader styles for players who want stronger lighting, atmosphere, and visual depth without replacing the pack's vanilla-oriented direction.
